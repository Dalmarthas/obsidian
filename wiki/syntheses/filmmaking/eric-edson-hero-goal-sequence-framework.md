# Question

What practical structure model does Eric Edson add to the vault?

# Answer

Edson adds a five-part structure model:

1. Treat structure as audience psychology rather than as studio bureaucracy.
   The source argues that story shape matters because human beings process change, surprise, and emotional meaning through patterned progression.

2. Build every story on three visible forces.
   A sympathetic active hero, a high-stakes external goal, and a powerful adversary create the minimum pressure system needed for screen drama.

3. Think of the three acts as three worlds.
   The story moves from ordinary life into a special world of heightened pressure and then into a mature final phase where the old plan is gone and the hero must act without safety.

4. Use the `Hero Goal Sequence` as the working unit of momentum.
   Instead of talking only in giant act terms, Edson gives the writer a concrete micro-unit: short-term goal, pursuit, fresh news, new direction.

5. Design major act turns as `Stunning Surprises`.
   The end of Act One and the end of Act Two should land as strong reversals that emotionally reframe the hero's path rather than as faint bookkeeping markers.

# Comparisons

- Compared with Sorkin, Edson is more prescriptive about structural units and act-turn design, while Sorkin is more focused on scene conflict and movement in performance.
- Compared with Truby, Edson is more comfortable with explicit structural scaffolds and numeric heuristics; Truby is more suspicious of shallow formula and more focused on deep story anatomy.
- Compared with the 2010 screenplay article, Edson offers a richer explanation of why structure works and a clearer micro-unit for diagnosing drift.
- Compared with Brown, Edson is less centered on suspense rhetoric and more centered on visible goal transitions as the engine of movement.

# Gaps

- The source is a secondary summary, not a primary transcript or direct text from Edson's own materials.
- The branch still lacks a serious counter-source defending looser or more anti-classical narrative forms on equal analytical terms.
- The vault now has a stronger structure-engineering layer, but still needs more scene-level emotional-architecture sources if it wants equal depth at micro and macro scales.
