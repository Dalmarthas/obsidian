# Question

What durable story-design model does the Truby source add to the current filmmaking branch?

# Answer

The Truby source adds a five-part architecture model:

1. Start with genre as real design logic.
   Genre is not a label attached after the fact. It defines the kind of story pressure, expectation, and payoff the script needs to satisfy and reinvent.

2. Test the premise before drafting.
   The writer should ask whether the story has a real desire line, a durable opponent, enough conflict, and a protagonist whose weakness can generate change.

3. Build structure from internal anatomy rather than formula worship.
   Truby is not anti-structure. He is anti-shallow structure. The point is to understand the deeper sequence of transformations and pressures rather than only mimic visible beats.

4. Let plot emerge from character weakness.
   The protagonist's flaw, need, or missing capacity should not be a decorative psychological note. It should create the conflicts and decisions that shape the narrative.

5. Front-load structural work.
   Use loglines, scene sequences, and rigorous criticism to solve foundational problems before dialogue polish and late-stage repair.

# Comparisons

- Compared with the 2010 screenplay source, Truby is less interested in page-count heuristics and more interested in whether the premise and anatomy are strong enough before pages even exist.
- Compared with Sorkin, Truby operates more at the pre-scene architecture layer, while Sorkin is stronger on scene conflict and dramatic motion in action.
- Compared with the Duffer Brothers, Truby is more explicit about genre and premise diagnostics, though both are strongly anti-drafting-too-early.
- Compared with Lynch, Truby is more systematizing and less intuition-led, making him a useful counterweight inside the branch.

# Gaps

- The current Truby layer comes from a secondary summary, not from a direct reading of his books or course material.
- The vault still lacks a richer canonical page on multi-step story anatomy beyond this one source.
- The next useful strengthening move would be a primary-source Truby ingest or a direct comparison between Truby and Sorkin on structure versus scene conflict.
