# Question

What durable preventive-health framework can be extracted from the current Michael Greger source?

# Answer

The current branch supports a six-part framework:

1. Treat diet as a major health cause, not as background decoration.
   The source argues that leading chronic diseases are heavily shaped by food patterns and lifestyle rather than by inevitability alone.

2. Make whole-food, plant-based eating the central default.
   The branch is strongest when interpreted as a whole-food pattern centered on plants rather than as a loose vegan label.

3. Think in root causes, not only in treatment after damage.
   Lifestyle medicine matters because it changes the upstream conditions generating chronic disease instead of only damping symptoms.

4. Operationalize the pattern daily.
   The `Daily Dozen` matters because a checklist is easier to execute than an abstract nutritional philosophy.

5. Protect the pattern from false shortcuts.
   Processed vegan junk food, vague moderation language, and overreliance on late-stage medical rescue all weaken the branch's logic.

6. Aim at healthspan, not just survival.
   The branch is really about preserving more years of strong function and reducing the years spent sick, limited, or metabolically damaged.

The strongest theme in the source is that prevention is not a moral accessory to medicine. It is one of the main places where health is actually made or lost.

# Comparisons

- Compared with [[wiki/concepts/health/sleep-quality|Sleep Quality]], this framework is more disease-prevention and nutrition-centered, while the Rhonda Patrick branch is more recovery- and timing-centered.
- Together, the two current health branches imply that durable health is built through repeated daily levers: food quality, sleep quality, behavioral timing, and better diagnosis.
- Compared with [[wiki/concepts/leadership/high-performance-mindset|High-Performance Mindset]], this branch is less about excellence under pressure and more about keeping the underlying biological system resilient enough to sustain function at all.

# Gaps

- The branch currently rests on one secondary summary rather than a deeper evidence stack or direct primary studies.
- The vault still needs stronger nutrition sources to test where the Greger framing is strongest, where it is overstated, and where other experts materially disagree.
- The `Daily Dozen` concept is operationally important, but the current source does not provide its full itemized detail.
