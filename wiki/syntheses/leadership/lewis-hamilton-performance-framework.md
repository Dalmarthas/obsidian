# Question

What durable performance framework can be extracted from the Lewis Hamilton masterclass summary?

# Answer

The current source base supports a six-part framework:

1. Want greatness strongly enough to organize around it.
   Desire and seriousness of aim are the starting conditions.

2. Prepare systemically.
   Review, scenario-plan, center yourself, and refine the routine constantly.

3. Treat failure as data.
   Write it down, shorten the recovery loop, and convert it into the next adjustment.

4. Use discipline without becoming brittle.
   Build habits, environmental supports, and clear goals, but leave room for flexibility.

5. Protect authenticity and balance.
   Being different is a strength, and diverse interests can prevent burnout.

6. Use success in service of something larger.
   Team responsibility and social platform responsibility are part of mature excellence.
