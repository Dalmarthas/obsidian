# Question

What durable team-leadership framework can be extracted from the Geno Auriemma masterclass summary?

# Answer

The current source base supports a six-part framework:

1. Put the team above the individual ego.
   The standard is collective excellence, not isolated talent.

2. Lead as service.
   Take hits, provide tools, and stay available rather than performing authority.

3. Build trust aggressively.
   Do not lie, do not give false hope, and do not break commitments.

4. Make accountability visible.
   Shared standards and direct correction reduce gossip and confusion.

5. Train above game conditions.
   Practice pressure should exceed performance pressure.

6. Distinguish fair from equal.
   Common standards can still require situational judgment.
