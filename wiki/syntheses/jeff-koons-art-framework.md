# Question

What durable art-making framework can be extracted from the Jeff Koons masterclass summary?

# Answer

The current source base supports a six-part framework:

1. Begin with acceptance.
   Remove judgment and treat your own history as valid material.

2. Remember that the viewer completes the work.
   Meaning is activated in encounter, not locked inside the object.

3. Use any source material that matters.
   Everyday objects, personal memory, and art history can all become serious art.

4. Think through the four core tools.
   Scale, color, form, and texture are the main levers of meaning.

5. Combine intuition with precision.
   Discovery and exact execution are not enemies.

6. Be generous and responsible.
   Care for detail is care for the viewer.
