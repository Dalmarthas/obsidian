# What It Is

An LLM Wiki is a persistent markdown knowledge base maintained by an LLM. Instead of answering only by retrieving fragments from raw files at query time, the system compiles durable pages that summarize, connect, and revise knowledge as new sources arrive. Main source: [[wiki/sources/2026-04-07-llm-wiki-idea|LLM Wiki Idea (Source Summary)]].

# Why It Matters

- It turns one-off retrieval into cumulative understanding.
- It lowers the maintenance cost that usually causes personal wikis to decay.
- It makes cross-links, contradictions, and synthesis durable artifacts that can be browsed directly in Obsidian.
