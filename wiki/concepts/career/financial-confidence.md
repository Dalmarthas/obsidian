# What It Is

Financial confidence is the practical sense that you have a workable plan for supporting yourself through a career shift, expansion, or leap of faith. In the current source base, it is contrasted with the fantasy of total financial security, which often keeps people stuck rather than prepared. Main source: [[wiki/sources/2026-04-07-building-a-career-elaine-welteroth-masterclass-complete-summary|Building a Career Elaine Welteroth Masterclass Complete Summary]].

# Why It Matters

- It makes reinvention less abstract and less terrifying.
- It pushes dream-building into contact with actual numbers and runway.
- It supports sustainable risk-taking rather than impulsive quitting or endless delay.
- It broadens the vault's career branch from inspiration into operational planning.

# Evidence In This Vault

- Set a savings target that supports the planned transition.
- Estimate required monthly and yearly numbers.
- Attach financial projections to blueprint or mind map lanes.
- Test revenue streams through side hustles before fully leaping.
- Shift from employee mentality toward CEO mentality when deciding where investment in help can unlock more growth.

# Tensions / Open Questions

- Planning can reduce uncertainty but cannot remove it entirely.
- The rhetoric of abundance can be clarifying, but it can also understate real financial fragility if used carelessly.
