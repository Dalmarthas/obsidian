# What It Is

Weak squares and outposts are squares that cannot easily be challenged by enemy pawns and therefore become stable platforms or targets in strategic play.

# Why It Matters

This is one of the clearest shifts from beginner play to stronger play. Instead of only asking what a piece attacks right now, the player starts asking which squares the position allows one side to occupy and exploit over time.
