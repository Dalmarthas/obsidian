# What It Is

Pawn breaks are pawn advances that challenge opposing pawns in order to open lines, alter the structure, or create new targets.

# Why It Matters

In many positions, especially closed ones, pawn breaks are the real mechanism that turns a plan into action. Without them, a side may have space or pressure but no way to convert it.
