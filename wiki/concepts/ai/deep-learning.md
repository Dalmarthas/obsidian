# What It Is

Deep learning is a machine-learning subfield built on multi-layer neural-network architectures that can learn complex patterns from data.

# Why It Matters

This is the layer where modern generative systems and LLMs become easier to situate. It provides the bridge between broad machine-learning ideas and the model families most people currently associate with AI products.

# Evidence In This Vault

- [[wiki/sources/2026-04-09-google-beginner-ai-course]] explains deep learning through artificial neural networks and places it underneath machine learning in the stack.
- The source also attributes broader pattern-recognition capacity and semi-supervised use cases to this layer.
- It frames both generative AI and LLMs as specializations inside the deep-learning layer.
