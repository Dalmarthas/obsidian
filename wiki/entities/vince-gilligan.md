# Overview

Vince Gilligan appears in this vault as a television writer-showrunner whose *Breaking Bad* method sharpens several existing filmmaking branches at once: genuine protagonist change, plot from psychology, strong antagonists, flash-forward pilot hooking, room-level authenticity checks, and theme discovered through story rather than preached in advance.

# Role In This Vault

- He strengthens the vault's television branch by rejecting static-character design in favor of real long-form transformation.
- He deepens the writing branch by making psychological honesty a plot rule instead of a flavor note.
- He adds a practical writers' room question that can be reused elsewhere:
  keep asking where the protagonist's head is actually at before deciding what happens next.
