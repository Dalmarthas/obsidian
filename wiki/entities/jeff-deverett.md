# Overview

Jeff Deverett enters the vault as an experienced producer-distributor voice focused less on pure film craft and more on the hard commercial path between a completed indie feature and an actual streaming deal. His main contribution is practical: how pitching, intermediaries, delivery packages, credibility, and persistence determine whether a film becomes platform-ready instead of merely finished.

# Role In This Vault

- He adds the strongest explicit distribution and platform-access layer currently present in the filmmaking branch.
- He sharpens the vault's understanding that commercial delivery is a separate problem from writing, directing, editing, or even locking picture.
- He connects filmmaking to buyer workflow, technical compliance, and reputation economics.
- He also strengthens the vault's pitch logic by showing how radically compressed decision windows change what a useful pitch looks like.
