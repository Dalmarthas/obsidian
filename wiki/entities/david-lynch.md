# Overview

David Lynch is presented in this vault as a filmmaker-teacher whose masterclass treats ideas, atmosphere, and artistic integrity as the governing forces of cinema. The current source frames him less as a general screenplay teacher than as a model of intuition-led, whole-system filmmaking.

# Role In This Vault

Lynch currently anchors the vault's material on catching ideas, protecting creative integrity, and understanding filmmaking as a coordinated act across writing, casting, design, image, and sound.
