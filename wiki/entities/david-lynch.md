# Overview

David Lynch is presented in this vault as a filmmaker-teacher whose branch now combines two layers: intuition-led creativity and a surprisingly direct practical screenwriting method. The current source base frames him as both a guardian of atmosphere and integrity and a maker who is comfortable using skeletal drafts and scene cards to keep a film visible while it is still forming.

# Role In This Vault

Lynch currently anchors the vault's material on catching ideas, protecting creative integrity, using light structure without suffocating intuition, and understanding filmmaking as a coordinated act across writing, casting, design, image, and sound.
