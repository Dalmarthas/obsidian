# Overview

Jeff Koons is presented in this vault as an art-and-creativity teacher whose work centers on self-acceptance, democratized source material, viewer participation, and the disciplined use of scale, color, form, and texture.

# Role In This Vault

Koons currently opens the vault's dedicated art branch and deepens the broader creativity branch through his emphasis on acceptance, generosity, and audience-completed meaning.
