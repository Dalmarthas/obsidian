# Overview

Jesse Cole enters this vault as a founder-operator who treats experience design, attention, experimentation, and belonging as one integrated business system. In the current source base, he is not just a sports promoter. He is a case study in how founder taste, anti-extraction economics, live-show design, creative cadence, and personal mission can combine into a brand people actively spread.

# Role In This Vault

- He gives the business branch a strong example of experience as product, not as marketing decoration.
- He sharpens the vault's language for fan-first strategy through `upper deck` empathy, friction removal, and generosity against category norms.
- He adds a clearer founder-operating-system layer through the `attention plan`, daily ideation, weekly idea labs, `LCP` reviews, and direct expectation-setting.
- He also links business strategy to a personal mission of belonging and care, making his case relevant to leadership and culture rather than to marketing alone.
- The newest source also sharpens his use of belief-signaling, gratitude rituals, and `culture symmetry` between internal employee experience and external fan magic.
