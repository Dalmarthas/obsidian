# Overview

Michael Hauge enters the vault as a screenwriting teacher focused on Act 1 foundation, protagonist empathy, visible external goals, and the warning that rushed setup and diffuse opening pages can sabotage the whole script.

# Role In This Vault

- He strengthens the vault's early-structure layer by focusing on what must be solid before the screenplay fully launches.
- He adds a clearer naming of `outer motivation` as the visible finish line that stabilizes the story.
- He sharpens the empathy-first side of character setup by arguing that connection should precede deeper flaw complexity.
- He provides a measured counterweight to advice that overvalues immediate acceleration at the expense of orientation.
