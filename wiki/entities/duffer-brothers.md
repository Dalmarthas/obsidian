# Overview

Matt and Ross Duffer are presented in this vault as television writers and showrunners whose masterclass turns the early development of a TV pilot into a highly staged, outline-led process. The current source uses Stranger Things as the main case study for their ideas about drive, planning, worldbuilding, and season design.

# Role In This Vault

The Duffer Brothers currently anchor the vault's strongest material on television-pilot construction, zoomed-out story development, and the practical craft of preventing a script from getting lost during development.
