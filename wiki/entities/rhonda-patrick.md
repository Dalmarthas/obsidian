# Overview

Rhonda Patrick enters this vault as a health-oriented source figure associated here with practical, evidence-framed sleep optimization. In the current branch, her role is not broad health philosophy but a specific, usable blend of circadian guidance, sleep troubleshooting, and cautious supplement use.

# Role In This Vault

- She anchors the first dedicated health branch in the vault.
- Her current source contributes a concrete protocol for improving sleep quality and reducing nighttime awakenings.
- The branch is especially useful because it bridges lifestyle guidance and medical triage instead of treating every sleep problem as the same problem.

# Evidence In This Vault

- The source connects sleep quality to light timing, darkness, temperature, stimulant timing, and repeated routines.
- It also makes diagnosis central by surfacing insomnia, sleep apnea, nocturia, menopause, and shift work as distinct problem contexts.
- The branch contributes a practical supplement layer while still treating supplements as secondary to better timing, environment, and cause-identification.
