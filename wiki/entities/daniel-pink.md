# Overview

Daniel Pink is presented in this vault as a teacher of humane performance and influence who connects persuasion, timing, motivation, and sustainable productivity. Across the current sources, he is less a `hack` thinker than a translator of behavioral research into practical operating principles.

# Role In This Vault

Pink now anchors two neighboring branches in the vault:

- persuasion, especially on attunement, rejection resilience, problem-finding, autonomy-support, and the role of timing in influence
- sustainable productivity, especially on ruthless prioritization, prime-hour protection, visible progress, restorative breaks, and consistency over intensity
