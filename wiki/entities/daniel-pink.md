# Overview

Daniel Pink is presented in this vault as a persuasion teacher who reframes influence as context design, perspective-taking, ethical service, and resilient action rather than manipulative selling.

# Role In This Vault

Pink now anchors the vault's persuasion branch, especially on attunement, rejection resilience, problem-finding, autonomy-support, and the role of timing in influence.
