# Overview

Dan Brown appears in this vault as a bestselling thriller novelist whose masterclass offers a practical system for commercial suspense writing. The current source associates him with *The Da Vinci Code*, *Angels and Demons*, *Inferno*, *Origin*, *Digital Fortress*, *Deception Point*, and *The Lost Symbol*. Main source: [[wiki/sources/2026-04-07-dan-brown-masterclass-complete-summary|Dan Brown Masterclass Complete Summary]].

# Role In This Vault

Brown functions here primarily as a craft reference for thriller construction rather than as a biographical subject. His current role is to anchor pages on suspense, the Three C's framework, narrative promises, and process discipline for long-form fiction.
