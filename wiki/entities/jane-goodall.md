# Overview

Jane Goodall appears in this vault as a primatologist, conservationist, and public advocate whose work connects animal cognition, environmental ethics, community-based conservation, and youth empowerment. In the current source base, her role is both scientific and moral: she challenges narrow conceptions of human exceptionalism while offering a practical model for hopeful action.
