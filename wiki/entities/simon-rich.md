# Overview

Simon Rich is presented in this vault as a comedy writer whose main contribution is not a rigid structural system but a repeatable premise-generation practice. His branch centers on idea abundance, playful frameworks, observation, and filtering rather than on waiting for rare inspiration.

# Role In This Vault

Rich currently anchors the vault's material on high-volume premise generation, comedy-oriented `what if?` thinking, and treating ideation as a skill that can be strengthened through repetition.
