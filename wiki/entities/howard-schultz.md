# Overview

Howard Schultz is presented in this vault as a leadership teacher and company builder whose model joins cultural investment, humane capitalism, operational discipline, crisis transparency, and offensive self-disruption.

# Role In This Vault

Schultz deepens the vault's business-leadership branch by adding a strong model for values-driven company building, culture as operating system, premium differentiation, and the relationship between crisis truth-telling and trust.
