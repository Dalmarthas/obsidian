# Overview

Goodby Silverstein & Partners appears in this vault as a creative advertising agency founded around the partnership of Jeff Goodby and Rich Silverstein. In the current source base, the agency is notable less for formal corporate process than for a specific philosophy: make work people want to see, respect the audience, trust makers, and build culture that enables risk, humor, and craft. Main source: [[wiki/sources/2026-04-07-advertising-goodby-silverstein-masterclass-summary|Goodby Silverstein Masterclass Summary]].

# Role In This Vault

This entity anchors the advertising branch of the wiki. It is the current source of record for humane advertising, mass intimacy, strategy-backed creativity, and agency culture built around trust and making.
