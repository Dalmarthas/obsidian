# Overview

George R.R. Martin appears in this vault as a fiction writer whose branch sharpens long-form series planning, adaptation divergence, historically grounded worldbuilding, agency-based POV design, and the preservation of narrative intent under fan and production pressure.

# Role In This Vault

- He deepens the fiction branch through more explicit worldbuilding, political realism, and multi-POV design.
- He adds a clear adaptation rule: the original work and the screen version are related but should not be forced into one continuity.
- He strengthens the worldview layer by defending narrative integrity against external pressure from fandom, speculation, and adaptation drift.
