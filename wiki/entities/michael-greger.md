# Overview

Michael Greger enters this vault as a physician-educator associated here with evidence-forward preventive nutrition and the case for whole-food, plant-based eating. In the current branch, his role is not generic wellness advice but a stronger structural claim: diet is a major root lever under chronic disease, healthspan, and lifestyle medicine.

# Role In This Vault

- He anchors the vault's first nutrition-centered health branch.
- His current source contributes a reusable framework that links food patterns to chronic-disease prevention, functional years of life, and root-cause care.
- The branch is especially useful because it combines theory, mechanism, practical checklists, and caveats about supplementation and processed food quality.

# Evidence In This Vault

- The source treats whole-food, plant-based nutrition as the default dietary pattern with the strongest benefit signal in the current branch.
- It ties diet to heart disease, diabetes, hypertension, kidney strain, and cancer risk rather than treating food as secondary lifestyle decoration.
- It frames lifestyle medicine as a root-cause alternative to symptom-only treatment for many chronic conditions.
- It operationalizes the branch through the `Daily Dozen` and through practical safeguards like `B12` and algae-derived omega-3s.
