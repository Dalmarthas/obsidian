# Overview

Aaron Sorkin is presented in this vault as a major screenwriter and teacher of dramatic craft. The current source base uses both his general masterclass summary and a more concrete *The Social Network* case study to map his approach to intention and obstacle, audience trust, dialogue precision, contested truth, and research-backed dramatization. Main sources: [[wiki/sources/2026-04-07-aaron-sorkin-masterclass-complete-summary|Aaron Sorkin Masterclass Complete Summary]], [[wiki/sources/2026-04-09-aaron-sorkin-on-the-social-network|Aaron Sorkin on The Social Network]].

# Role In This Vault

Sorkin functions here as a craft reference point rather than a biographical subject. His ideas currently anchor the vault's pages on intention and obstacle, audience trust, dialogue musicality, research-backed dramatization, scene propulsion, rewriting, and conflicting-perspectives design.
