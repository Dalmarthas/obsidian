# Overview

Aaron Sorkin is presented in this vault as a major screenwriter and teacher of dramatic craft. The current source describes him as the writer behind *The West Wing*, *The Social Network*, *A Few Good Men*, and *Steve Jobs*, and uses his masterclass as a compact operating model for dialogue-driven dramatic writing. Main source: [[wiki/sources/2026-04-07-aaron-sorkin-masterclass-complete-summary|Aaron Sorkin Masterclass Complete Summary]].

# Role In This Vault

Sorkin functions here as a craft reference point rather than a biographical subject. His ideas currently anchor the vault's pages on intention and obstacle, audience trust, dialogue musicality, scene propulsion, and rewriting.
