# Overview

Cal Newport appears in this vault as a career strategist who treats satisfying work as something built through scarce skill, not discovered through mood alone. In the current source base, he functions as the strongest explicit critic of `follow your passion` thinking and as a translator between deliberate practice and career leverage.

# Role In This Vault

Newport now anchors a stricter career-capital branch inside the business domain. His role is to make career design less romantic and more compounding:

- distinguish satisfying work from early excitement
- define creativity, control, and impact as earned job conditions
- treat rare and valuable skill as the currency that buys those conditions
- frame the craftsman mindset as the daily posture that keeps skill compounding
- connect deliberate practice to real career leverage rather than to abstract improvement alone
