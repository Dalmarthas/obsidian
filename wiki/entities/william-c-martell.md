# Overview

William C. Martell is a screenwriter and writing teacher whose current branch in this vault centers on the question `How is this my story?` and on the practical need to find a real emotional doorway into any assignment, genre piece, or commercial project.

# Role In This Vault

- He sharpens the vault's creative-integrity layer inside professional assignment writing.
- He adds a practical test for whether a writer should accept, reshape, or decline a project.
- He connects worldview and authenticity to the day-to-day economics of screenwriting rather than leaving them as abstract artistic values.
