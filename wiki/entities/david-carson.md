# Overview

David Carson is presented in this vault as a designer-teacher whose work reframes graphic design as emotional, intuitive, and human rather than merely orderly or legible. The current source treats him as a major reference point for expressive typography and anti-safe visual communication.

# Role In This Vault

Carson currently anchors the vault's new design branch, especially the pages on graphic design, typography as image, and the role of risk and human presence in creative work.
