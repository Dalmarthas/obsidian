# Overview

Salman Rushdie is presented in this vault as a fiction teacher whose craft model joins literary ambition, narrative freedom, rhythmic language, global reading, self-knowledge, and rigorous revision.

# Role In This Vault

Rushdie now anchors the vault's broader fiction-writing branch, especially on openings, point of view, place, worldview, surrealist permission, and the long arc from conception to revision.
