# Overview

Martin Scorsese is presented in this vault as a filmmaking teacher whose model joins vocation, cinema history, visual literacy, rigorous preparation, trust-driven collaboration, and discovery through editing.

# Role In This Vault

Scorsese expands the vault's filmmaking branch beyond atmosphere and integrity into film language, historical awareness, casting, editorial structure, and the relationship between preparation and openness during production.
