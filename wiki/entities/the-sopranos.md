# Overview

*The Sopranos* appears in this vault as a television case study in moral ambiguity, anti-formula storytelling, thematic episode structure, psychologically charged therapy scenes, and writers' room method that turns lived anecdote into durable dramatic material.

# Role In This Vault

This entity functions as a show-level reference point rather than as a fandom page. It anchors reusable lessons about how prestige television can hold contradictory characters, multiple simultaneous storylines, and audience discomfort without collapsing into formula or neat moral reassurance.
