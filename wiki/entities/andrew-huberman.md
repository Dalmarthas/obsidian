# Overview

Andrew Huberman appears in this vault as a translator of neurobiology into practical daily routines. In the current source base, his role is not broad neuroscience education but a specific operating model for how morning sequencing can influence circadian timing, alertness, focus, and training readiness.

# Role In This Vault

Huberman currently anchors a health-and-productivity crossover branch focused on:

- using light as a timing signal
- delaying caffeine as an energy-stability tactic
- protecting early deep-work blocks
- linking physiology, focus, and training into one day-start sequence
