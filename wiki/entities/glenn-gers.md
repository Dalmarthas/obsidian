# Overview

Glenn Gers enters the vault as a screenwriting teacher whose current contribution centers on question-driven story design, character pursuit through obstacles, theory-of-mind characterization, and a flexible, self-aware writing process.

# Role In This Vault

- He adds one of the vault's clearest universal diagnostic tools for story construction.
- He sharpens the bridge between premise design and scene-level writing by showing that the same questions can operate at multiple scales.
- He strengthens the character branch by insisting that every character is trying to be the main character of their own story.
- He gives the process branch a more adaptive, anti-dogmatic position on workflow and writer's block.
