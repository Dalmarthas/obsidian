# Overview

Johnny Harris is presented in this vault as a knowledge-communication creator whose branch centers on visual-first explanation, anchor-bridge pacing, curiosity-before-context sequencing, research-backed explainer design, timeline rhythm, and team-scale YouTube documentary production.

# Role In This Vault

Harris gives the vault a concrete model for explainers and educational storytelling that sits between communication, visual literacy, audience guidance, and nonfiction video production. His branch is not mainly about persuasion through debate or negotiation. It is about how to make information felt, then understood, and how to build a production system around that experience.
